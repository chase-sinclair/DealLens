Use these benchmarks to evaluate Phase 3 prompt outputs and Phase 4 Zapier mapping.

```text
Strong fit
Recommended next step: Proceed to initial review
Fit score range: 78-88
Data completeness range: 80%-90%
```

- Detailed customer revenue by year
- Contract renewal schedule
- Technician utilization by region
