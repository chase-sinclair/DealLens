# Northstar Field Services - Confidential Information Memorandum

## Transaction Overview

Hamilton Ridge Advisors has been retained to explore a majority recapitalization of Northstar Field Services, a founder-owned provider of recurring facilities maintenance services for multi-site commercial customers.

The company is seeking a private equity partner to support geographic expansion, add-on acquisitions, and professionalization of the management team.

## Business Model

Northstar provides route-density field services for regional property managers, healthcare facilities, light industrial operators, and multi-site commercial accounts. Services include preventative maintenance, emergency repair, janitorial support, minor HVAC coordination, and vendor-managed field response.

The revenue model is built around recurring monthly maintenance contracts, with supplemental project revenue tied to seasonal work orders and emergency repairs.

## Customer Base

The company serves approximately 185 active customers. The top customer represents 11% of FY2025 revenue, and the top five customers represent 34%. Management reports customer retention above 91% over the last three fiscal years.

## Investment Highlights

- Recurring revenue from contractual facilities maintenance.
- Fragmented regional market with clear add-on acquisition potential.
- Dense service routes that support margin expansion.
- Founder succession opportunity with a second-layer management team already in place.
- Opportunity to add specialized trades such as HVAC, electrical, and plumbing coordination.

## Growth Opportunities

- Expand into adjacent states through local tuck-in acquisitions.
- Increase wallet share with healthcare and light industrial customers.
- Improve technician scheduling and route optimization.
- Add national account sales coverage.

## Key Risks

- Labor availability may constrain expansion.
- Customer concentration needs deeper review.
- Add-on acquisition integration capability is not yet proven.

## Preliminary Diligence Requests

- Customer revenue by year for the top 20 customers.
- Contract terms and renewal schedule.
- Technician utilization, turnover, and wage trend by market.
- Acquisition pipeline and integration playbook.
