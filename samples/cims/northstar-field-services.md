## 6. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
Northstar provides essential facilities maintenance services through a high-density regional route model. The Company serves **300+ active customer sites** with a focus on interior and exterior maintenance consistency.

**Products and Services:**
* **HVAC Maintenance (40% of Revenue):** Quarterly preventive maintenance and 24/7 emergency repairs.
* **Janitorial Services (35% of Revenue):** Daily and weekly commercial cleaning.
* **Exterior Care (25% of Revenue):** Landscaping and lot maintenance.

**Customers and Contracting:**
