## 5. OWNERSHIP, HISTORY, AND MANAGEMENT
**History:** BrightCart was founded in 2009 as a local boutique in Portland, Oregon. Over the subsequent decade, the Company expanded its footprint through an owned ecommerce platform and a regional wholesale network. The business has remained under original family ownership since inception.

**Management Team:** The Company is led by the founding family members who oversee merchandising and retail operations. The Company lacks a formal professional management layer in finance and digital marketing, with many key functions handled by long-tenured staff members rather than specialized executives.

**Employees:** BrightCart employs approximately **95 people**, including retail staff at two physical locations, warehouse and fulfillment personnel, and a small corporate team in Portland.

## 6. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
BrightCart designs and sells a curated selection of premium household goods. The Company utilizes a multi-channel distribution strategy to reach its target demographic of high-income, eco-conscious homeowners.

**Products and Services:**

* **Textiles:** Premium bedding, towels, and linens.
* **Kitchenware:** High-end artisanal cooking tools and storage solutions.
* **Home Decor:** Niche decorative accents and stationery.
* **Wholesale Distribution:** Sales to 50+ boutique retail partners.

**Customers and Contracting:**
The revenue model is primarily transactional, with over 90% of sales occurring as one-time purchases. The Company lacks a formal subscription or loyalty-based recurring revenue stream. Wholesale relationships are governed by individual purchase orders rather than long-term volume commitments.
