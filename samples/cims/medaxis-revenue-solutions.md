# MedAxis Revenue Solutions - Confidential Information Memorandum

## Transaction Overview

Clearwater Partners is advising MedAxis Revenue Solutions on a potential sale process. MedAxis is a sponsor-backed technology-enabled revenue cycle management provider serving outpatient clinics and specialty physician groups.

The seller is seeking a new majority investor. The transaction is positioned as a platform opportunity for healthcare services investors with experience in provider workflow and outsourced billing operations.

## Business Model

MedAxis supports specialty physician practices with claims submission, denial management, coding review, payment posting, and analytics. The company combines offshore processing, domestic account managers, and a proprietary workflow dashboard.

Revenue is generated through monthly recurring service fees plus performance-based collections fees. The company reports that approximately 72% of FY2025 revenue is recurring or repeat in nature.

## Customer Base

MedAxis serves more than 90 provider groups. The top customer represents 18% of FY2025 revenue, while the top five customers represent 49%. The CIM states that retention is strong but does not disclose net revenue retention by customer cohort.

## Investment Highlights

- Attractive EBITDA margin profile.
- Durable demand for outsourced provider revenue cycle support.
- Specialty practice focus creates workflow expertise.
- Analytics module may support cross-sell and pricing expansion.

## Growth Opportunities

- Expand into adjacent specialties.
- Upsell analytics and denial-prevention modules.
- Improve automation for lower-complexity claims.
- Pursue tuck-in acquisitions of specialty billing providers.

## Key Risks

- Customer concentration is elevated.
- Sponsor-backed ownership may mean easy operational improvements have already been captured.
- Net revenue retention is not disclosed.
- Offshore delivery model requires compliance and quality control diligence.

## Preliminary Diligence Requests

- Net revenue retention by customer cohort.
- Customer contract renewal schedule.
- Revenue by specialty and service line.
- Offshore delivery quality metrics and compliance procedures.
