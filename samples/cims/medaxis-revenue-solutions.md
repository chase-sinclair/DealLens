## 6. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
MedAxis provides end-to-end revenue cycle support for specialty physician groups, outpatient clinics, ambulatory surgery centers (ASCs), and provider networks. The Company's technology-enabled approach streamlines the bridge between clinical documentation and financial reimbursement.

**Products and Services:**

* **Claims Management:** End-to-end submission and payment posting.
* **Denial Management:** Tech-driven identification and resolution of payer denials.
* **Coding Review:** Specialty-specific clinical coding to ensure compliance and revenue integrity.
* **Analytics Platform:** Proprietary dashboard providing real-time visibility into financial KPIs.

**Customers and Contracting:** Approximately **72%** of FY2025 revenue is recurring or repeat in nature, derived from monthly recurring service fees and performance-based collections fees. While the Company maintains long-standing relationships with high-volume provider networks, customer concentration is elevated, with the top five customers representing **49%** of total revenue.
